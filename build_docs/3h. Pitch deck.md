## Slide 2 — The Problem

**India has 1.4 billion people. Most of them cannot access the legal system that exists to protect them.**

Three hard facts:

- A basic legal consultation costs ₹2,000–₹10,000. 800 million Indians live on under ₹10,000 a month.
- 50 million new legal cases are filed every year. 50 million more sit pending in backlog.
- In 2023–24, India replaced its three most foundational criminal laws — IPC, CrPC, and the Indian Evidence Act — with BNS, BNSS, and BSA. Most citizens have never heard of them. Most lawyers are still recalibrating.

**The result:** Crimes go unreported. Rights go unexercised. Justice goes undelivered — not because the law failed, but because access failed.

This is not a social problem dressed as a business opportunity. It is a market failure at the scale of a billion people.

## Slide 4 — The Solution

**One input. One expert-grade output. No lawyer required to begin.**

A citizen describes what happened to them — in plain Hindi, Hinglish, or English — the way they would explain it to a trusted friend.

NyayaSetu returns:

- The top 5–10 most relevant clauses across BNS, BNSS, and BSA
- Exact statutory text for each clause
- Plain-language explanation of what each clause means
- Why each clause applies to their specific situation
- A recommended next step: file FIR, approach lawyer, file complaint

For citizens who need to act immediately, NyayaSetu generates a **paid structured writeup** — FIR-ready and lawyer-ready — that they can walk into any police station or law firm with directly.

**This is not a chatbot. It is a legal intelligence tool — the digital entry point to the Indian justice system.**

## Slide 6 — Technology

**Built on open-source infrastructure. Designed for national scale.**

The core is a multi-node agentic pipeline — not a chatbot, not a search engine. Every component is purpose-selected:

|Layer|Technology|Why|
|---|---|---|
|Orchestration|LangGraph|Multi-node agentic pipeline with human-in-the-loop|
|Vector retrieval|Qdrant + multilingual-e5-large|Hindi/Hinglish/English semantic search over BNS/BNSS/BSA|
|Graph intelligence|Neo4j + GraphRAG|Clause relationship mapping — finds what vector search misses|
|LLM|Groq + Llama 3 → Claude API|Fast, free for MVP — Claude for production accuracy|
|Backend|FastAPI + PostgreSQL|Production-grade, async, horizontally scalable|
|Frontend|Next.js|Mobile-first, India-grade UX|
|Payments|Razorpay|UPI-native — the payment rail India actually uses|

**Key technical differentiator:** NyayaSetu never hallucinates a legal reference. The synthesis node is retrieval-grounded — it can only explain clauses that were retrieved from the corpus. Every clause reference is verified at the node level before it reaches the citizen.

**Human safety net:** Low-confidence or high-stakes cases (life, liberty, serious harm) are automatically routed to a verified evaluator lawyer dashboard. No AI decision is final when the stakes are existential.
